  Timestamp extension flag for In Situ Operations, Administration, and
                    Maintenance (IOAM) Direct Export
                 draft-ahuang-ippm-dex-timestamp-ext-00

Abstract

   This document defines a new extension flag for In Situ Operations,
   Administration, and Maintenance (IOAM) Direct Exporting.  The new
   flag allows IOAM DEX Option-type to add the timestamp into the
   header.

Requirements Language

   The key words "MUST", "MUST NOT", "REQUIRED", "SHALL", "SHALL NOT",
   "SHOULD", "SHOULD NOT", "RECOMMENDED", "NOT RECOMMENDED", "MAY", and
   "OPTIONAL" in this document are to be interpreted as described in BCP
   14 [RFC2119] [RFC8174] when, and only when, they appear in all
   capitals, as shown here.
